About Michael Achumba

Michael Achumba is a learning and development and CAPM certified project management professional with over 3 years leading learning projects in organizations. His skills include training coordination, administration and facilitation. He believes that learning and development is a driver for improving individual performance in every sector.

Early Life

Michael was born to Engr Innocent and Mrs Beatrice Achumba on 3rd June 1994 at Bendel Clinic, Enerhen, Warri, Delta State, Nigeria. He was the second of four children born to the Innocent Achumba's family. He spent most of his childhood in transition attending over 10 schools in 5 different cities (Warri, Abuja, Benin, Aba, and Lagos) over the course of his primary and secondary education.
He attended Federal Polytechnic Nekede, Owerri for his National Diploma in Electrical/Electronic Engineering before bagging his Bachelors of Engineering in the same course from Federal University of Technology, Owerri in 2018.

Professional Experience

During his compulsory national service year with the National Youth Service Corps, he decided to give back to the community by teaching in a senior secondary school over the course of the year. At this time, LEAP Africa, an NGO began a fellowship for corp members to facilitate mentorship of secondary school students over the course of 3 years. He gladly joined the iLEAD fellowship and serve in a public secondary school in Kuje, FCT, Nigeria. The experience completely changed his perspective on the educational system
